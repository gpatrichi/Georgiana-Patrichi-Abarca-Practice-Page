Georgiana's Romanian Meatball Soup

Traditional Recipe with a Twist






The History of the Soup

This soup is very yummy. My mom used to make it every summer when I was a kid. It's hydrating and full of veggies and protein, which makes it a very healthy soup.

Directions

Steps for Prepping the Veggies:

  1. Wash, clean, peel, and chop the vegetables (a food processor can help).
  2. Add all the veggies to a large pot.
  3. Add the lovage and black pepper.
  4. Add 3 liters of water to the pot.
  5. Set stove to High and bring to a boil.

Steps for Prepping the Meatballs:

  1. Finely dice the parsley and dill.
  2. Mix turkey, bread crumbs, rice, egg, herbs, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Mix well by hand or with gloves.
  4. Form 2-inch meatballs (a melon scooper can help).
  5. Gently place meatballs in the boiling soup one by one.

Steps for Cooking:

  1. Allow all ingredients to come to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and cover with a lid.
  3. Cook for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. After cooking, add salt and lemon juice and stir well.
  5. Let sit for 10 minutes. Serve with yogurt, hot pepper, and bread.

Enjoy!
